## Fluid biomarker papers

- **Why it applies:** Human or other living-organism studies that collect biofluids and evaluate biomarker utility for clinically relevant or pre-clinically informative purposes.
- **Status:** needs_work

- **Study registration and analytic plan disclosure** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The current manuscript pack does not yet name a registry, preregistration record, or pre-specified analytic plan.
  - Needed next: Prepare the Transparency, Rigor and Reproducibility Summary to state the actual registration status and whether the analytic plan was pre-specified.
- **Participant and sample accounting** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The pack already names endotype context and clinically relevant readouts, but it does not yet enumerate screened, sampled, analyzed, quality-passed, and outcome-assessed participants.
  - Needed next: Add a CONSORT-style accounting block for screened, sampled, analyzed, QC-passed, and clinically assessed participants.
- **Biofluid sample handling, batching, and blinding** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The pack records sample types and the planned readout lane, but it does not yet capture acquisition timing, handling conditions, batch assignment, freeze-thaw history, or blinded QC roles.
  - Needed next: Capture collection timing, subject state, handling/storage conditions, batch assignment, technical failures, and who was blinded during sample handling and quality control.
- **Assay validation and analytical performance** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The biomarker panel and readout spine are defined, but assay CV, LLOQ/LLOD, batch reproducibility, dilutional linearity, specificity, and spike-recovery are not yet in the manuscript pack.
  - Needed next: Name the assay platform, validation status, replicates, CV, quantitation limits, linearity, specificity, and lot-bridging behavior for the primary biomarker measurements.
- **Clinically relevant assessment validity** [partial critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The pack ties the biomarker lane to endotypes and downstream burden, but it does not yet document the validity and reliability of the paired clinical assessments.
  - Needed next: State which inclusion criteria and outcome measures are established standards, emerging standards, or still provisional, and cite the validation basis.
- **Statistics, multiple comparisons, and replication** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: No manuscript-facing declaration yet covers statistical assumptions, multiple-comparison handling, or the status of internal/external replication.
  - Needed next: Add the planned statistical assumptions, multiple-comparison correction path, outlier/missing-data handling, and the current replication or external-validation status.
- **Data, code, and sample availability** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The pack has a source manifest, but it does not yet state repository, DOI, code availability, or future-use permissions for biofluid samples.
  - Needed next: Draft the data-availability, analytic-code, and sample-availability statements expected by the JNT biomarker checklist.
