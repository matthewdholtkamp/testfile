## Fluid biomarker papers

- **Why it applies:** Human or other living-organism studies that collect biofluids and evaluate biomarker utility for clinically relevant or pre-clinically informative purposes.
- **Status:** needs_work

- **Study registration and analytic plan disclosure** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The current manuscript pack does not yet name a registry, preregistration record, or pre-specified analytic plan.
  - Needed next: Prepare the Transparency, Rigor and Reproducibility Summary to state the actual registration status and whether the analytic plan was pre-specified.
- **Participant and sample accounting** [partial critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The pack already names endotype context and clinically relevant readouts, but it does not yet enumerate screened, sampled, analyzed, quality-passed, and outcome-assessed participants.
  - Needed next: Add a CONSORT-style accounting block for screened, sampled, analyzed, QC-passed, and clinically assessed participants.
- **Biofluid sample handling, batching, and blinding** [partial critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The pack records sample types and the planned readout lane, but it does not yet capture acquisition timing, handling conditions, batch assignment, freeze-thaw history, or blinded QC roles.
  - Needed next: Capture collection timing, subject state, handling/storage conditions, batch assignment, technical failures, and who was blinded during sample handling and quality control.
- **Assay validation and analytical performance** [partial critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The biomarker panel and readout spine are defined, but assay CV, LLOQ/LLOD, batch reproducibility, dilutional linearity, specificity, and spike-recovery are not yet in the manuscript pack.
  - Needed next: Name the assay platform, validation status, replicates, CV, quantitation limits, linearity, specificity, and lot-bridging behavior for the primary biomarker measurements.
- **Clinically relevant assessment validity** [partial critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The pack ties the biomarker lane to endotypes and downstream burden, but it does not yet document the validity and reliability of the paired clinical assessments.
  - Needed next: State which inclusion criteria and outcome measures are established standards, emerging standards, or still provisional, and cite the validation basis.
- **Statistics, multiple comparisons, and replication** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: No manuscript-facing declaration yet covers statistical assumptions, multiple-comparison handling, or the status of internal/external replication.
  - Needed next: Add the planned statistical assumptions, multiple-comparison correction path, outlier/missing-data handling, and the current replication or external-validation status.
- **Data, code, and sample availability** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The pack has a source manifest, but it does not yet state repository, DOI, code availability, or future-use permissions for biofluid samples.
  - Needed next: Draft the data-availability, analytic-code, and sample-availability statements expected by the JNT biomarker checklist.

## Pathophysiological mechanistic studies

- **Why it applies:** Systematic, controlled in vivo or in vitro experimental manipulations intended to advance fundamental mechanistic knowledge relevant to traumatic brain or spinal cord injury.
- **Status:** needs_work

- **Study registration and analytic plan disclosure** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The current manuscript pack does not yet identify a preregistration record or explicit analytic-plan declaration.
  - Needed next: Prepare the Transparency, Rigor and Reproducibility Summary to state whether the mechanistic study was preregistered and whether the analytic plan was pre-specified.
- **Subject accounting, randomization, and blinding** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The pack does not yet describe subject accounting, randomization adequacy, or who was blinded during manipulations and analyses.
  - Needed next: Add subject accounting plus explicit randomization and blinding language, or state clearly why those elements were not used.
- **Experimental manipulation and batching details** [partial critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The current pack names the mechanistic next test (Run an NLRP3-centered panel with cytokines, GFAP, and secondary tau-amplification readouts across acute, subacute, and chronic windows.) and translational target (NLRP3), but it does not yet capture timing, state, devices, batching, or technical-failure details.
  - Needed next: Capture manipulation timing, subject state, devices or assays used, batching rules, storage conditions, and unexpected events for the lead mechanistic experiment.
- **Target engagement, specificity, and control logic** [partial critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The current packet already points at NLRP3 and the next experimental move, but target engagement, specificity controls, cross-validation, and positive/negative controls are not yet explicit.
  - Needed next: Add target-engagement readouts, specificity controls, cross-validation, and negative/positive control language to the mechanistic plan.
- **Primary-analysis assumptions and multiple-comparison management** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The manuscript pack does not yet state assumptions, outlier rules, missing-data handling, or how multiple comparisons will be controlled.
  - Needed next: Document statistical assumptions, independence handling, outlier and missing-data rules, and the multiple-comparison strategy for the mechanistic analyses.
- **Replication and external validation status** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The pack does not yet say whether the mechanistic result has internal replication, external validation, or only a planned replication path.
  - Needed next: State whether replication is complete, ongoing, planned, or absent, and register that plan when possible.
- **Data, code, and sample availability** [missing critical]
  - Evidence in current pack: The current pack does not yet declare where data, code, or residual samples will be shared or requested.
  - Needed next: Draft the data-availability, analytic-code, and sample-availability statements expected by the JNT mechanistic checklist.
